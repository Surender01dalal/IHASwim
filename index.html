The Indian Hydrophilic Academy (IHA) is a prominent company in North India that was established in 2010. IHA is renowned for its expertise in the running, installation, maintenance, and operation of swimming pools and filtration plants. The academy is duly registered and holds its own PF, ESI, and GST. It is committed to providing comprehensive services, ensuring the efficient management and renovation of swimming pools and filtration plants.

IHA boasts a highly skilled team comprising qualified coaches, lifeguards, and certified plant operators. In addition to its core services, IHA is well-known for offering swimming classes tailored for both beginners and providing advanced training for experienced swimmers. Over the past fourteen years, IHA has successfully managed more than 50 swimming pools, including both Olympic-sized and Semi-Olympic-sized, across Delhi, NCR, and North India.

    Mr. Surender Pal Dalal, an esteemed ex-serviceman of the Indian armed forces, brings to the table over two decades of valuable experience. In addition to his military background, he is a government-recognized swimming contractor. Mr. Dalal's expertise in swimming led him to establish the Indian Hydrophilic Academy in 2010, showcasing his commitment to promoting aquatic skills.
    Having been proficient in swimming since childhood, Mr. Dalal's passion for the sport transcended into active participation in university games during his academic years. This commitment to swimming continued seamlessly into his service in the armed forces, where he not only demonstrated his proficiency but also contributed significantly to the aquatic domain. His background reflects a rich blend of military discipline, entrepreneurial spirit, and a deep-rooted connection to the world of swimming.
